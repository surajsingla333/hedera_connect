1) Unzip the folder (which you already did since you are reading this file)
2) Open Google Chrome
3) Type `chrome://extensions/` on the address bar
4) On the top left corner, you will see a button `Load Unpacked`
5) Click on `Load Unpacked` button
6) Goto the extract folder
7) Select the `build` folder
8) Click OK
9) The extension will be added on the chrome browser.
